How StegoSafe Works

🚀 Step 1: You upload a PNG image (lossless!).

🔒 Step 2: Enter your secret message and an optional password to encrypt it.

🧬 Step 3: StegoSafe encodes your message into the least significant bits of each pixel.

🖼️ Step 4: Download the new image—looks unchanged but holds your hidden data!

🔍 Decoding: Upload the stego-image, enter password if used, and reveal your secret. 🎉

⚠️ Remember: Use PNG image and keep your password safe!
